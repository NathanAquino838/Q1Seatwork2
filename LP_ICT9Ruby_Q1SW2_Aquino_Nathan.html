Characters of Noli Me Tangere
Maria Clara She is a woman of high social standing who is thought to be Captain Tiago's daughter and Father Damaso's goddaughter. She is, in fact, Father Damaso's biological daughter, the result of a scandalous relationship between the old priest and Captain Tiago's wife.Why It’s Time To Rewrite María Clara & Our Filipina Story. Padre Damaso Dámaso, Padre. Dámaso Verdolagas (commonly known as Padre Damaso or Father Damaso), a Franciscan, was the former curate of San Diego's parish church. He was the curate for nearly two decades before being replaced by the much younger Padre Salvi. Padre Damaso. (n.d.). Noli Me Tangere Wiki. https://noli-me-tangere.fandom.com/wiki/Padre_Damaso
Elias An outlaw and vagabond revolutionary who despises the Catholic Church and the Spanish government's control over the Philippines. Elas swears to protect the young man from his enemies after Ibarra saves his life from a vicious crocodile.Elias.(n.d.). Noli Me Tangere Wiki. https://noli-me-tangere.fandom.com/wiki/Elias
Captain Tiago Capitan Tiago was a powerful businessman in San Diego and Maria Clara's father. Capitan Tiago struggled to obey the friars' will when he married his daughter to Crisostomo Ibarra.Capitan Tiago. (n.d.). Noli Me Tangere Wiki. https://noli-me-tangere.fandom.com/wiki/Capitan_Tiago
Padre Salvi Padre Salvi was the town curate of San Diego and the successor of Padre Damaso. Possessing a rivalry with the town's alferez, he later orchestrated the downfall of Crisostomo Ibarra, manipulating the townspeople in the process.Padre Salvi. (n.d.).
